I am a Graduate Student (Ph.D. Candidate) in the Department of Intelligent Electronics and Computer Engineering at Chonnam National University, Gwangju, South Korea. My research focuses on developing intelligent and trustworthy Artificial Intelligence techniques for next-generation communication networks, with an emphasis on Graph Neural Networks, Knowledge Graphs, Network Digital Twins, and Failure Localization in 5G and beyond wireless systems.

I received my B.Sc. from Kano University of Science and Technology, Nigeria, and my M.Sc. in Computer Science from Jodhpur National University, Rajasthan, India. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Chonnam National University, where my research explores knowledge-guided graph learning and intelligent network management for future communication systems.

My current research investigates heterogeneous knowledge graph representation learning, graph neural networks, network digital twins, failure diagnosis and localization, explainable artificial intelligence, and intelligent network automation. I am particularly interested in designing scalable and interpretable AI frameworks for reliable operation of 5G and future 6G core networks.

My publications span areas including Graph Neural Networks, Knowledge Graphs, Network Digital Twins, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and intelligent network management, with applications to failure diagnosis, cybersecurity, and autonomous communication systems.

Beyond research, I am passionate about collaborative research, mentoring, and developing practical AI solutions that bridge the gap between academic research and real-world network deployment. I welcome opportunities for research collaboration and academic exchange. If you would like to discuss research ideas or potential collaborations, please feel free to contact me.

Research Interests

My research aims to develop intelligent, explainable, and trustworthy AI methods for communication networks by integrating graph learning, knowledge representation, and network intelligence.

Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge graph construction, reasoning, representation learning, and semantic modeling for intelligent communication networks.

Graph Neural Networks

Heterogeneous graph neural networks, graph attention mechanisms, spectral graph learning, and relation-aware message passing.

Network Digital Twins

AI-driven digital twins for intelligent monitoring, failure prediction, fault diagnosis, and autonomous network management.

Failure Localization

Knowledge graph-based failure localization, root-cause analysis, telemetry analytics, and relation-aware propagation in 5G core networks.

Artificial Intelligence

Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Explainable AI, representation learning, and trustworthy intelligent systems.

5G/6G Intelligent Networks

Intelligent network management, network automation, resource optimization, and resilient communication systems for next-generation mobile networks.
